About Me

Organizational transformation strategist dedicated to building more equitable communities

Summary

I specialize in guiding organizations through transformational change by integrating Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) principles into strategic planning, facilitation, change management, and program evaluation. With experience spanning nonprofits, government agencies, and multinational organizations, I design and lead initiatives that foster belonging, build capacity, and align teams toward equity-driven outcomes. My work combines global perspectives, innovative approaches, and actionable strategies to inspire new thinking and create sustainable impact.
